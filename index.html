Who We Are

Built by Riders,
For Riders

Squamish, BC

Shred Shed Repairs was created in 2021 when three friends working in different industries decided to join forces and build something special.

We saw an opportunity to create a welcoming, full-service repair facility that could serve Squamish's growing motorsport and vehicle maintenance needs. Our mechanics are riders too — we understand your machines because we live and breathe the same lifestyle.

With Red Seal certified mechanics trained across multiple vehicle types and motorsport brands, we deliver fast turnarounds without cutting corners.
